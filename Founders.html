About Us

We are a group of enthusiastic 10th graders, studying in SVKM JV Parekh International School, passionate about cultural diversity and the preservation of our global heritage, so when our IGCSE curiculum gave us the opportunity to spread awareness regarding the same through our Global Perspectives Team Project, we did not hesitate. United by our shared interest in languages and cultures, we have embarked on this project to make a meaningful contribution to the protection and celebration of indigenous languages. Our mission is to spread awareness about the importance of indigenous languages and their invaluable role in maintaining cultural diversity. We believe that every language is a treasure trove of knowledge, traditions, and unique worldviews, and it is our responsibility to ensure these languages continue to thrive.
