Survey 1

In one of our surveys, the most essential question we asked was to
know how much people knew about the term "Indigenous Languages".
Unfortunately, 83.4% of responders had no knowledge or very
little knowledge about the topic of our project. Using this
knowledge, we were able to plan and stream line our awareness
projects to aim for most people to gain enough knowledge to
care for languages as a whole
