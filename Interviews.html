Interview

We had the privilege of interviewing Mr. Bin Mohamed Fazil,
an expert in indigenous languages who works at UNESCO.
With a deep understanding of endangered languages and cultural preservation,
Mr. Bin Mohamed Fazil has dedicated his career to advocating for the
recognition and revitalization of indigenous languages around the world.
In our conversation, he emphasized the importance of spreading
awareness through education, technology, and community involvement.
His work at UNESCO focuses on global initiatives that support language
revitalization efforts
