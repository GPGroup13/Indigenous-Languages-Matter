How We Spread Awareness

To spread awareness about indigenous languages, we utilized a variety of platforms and
tools to engage a wide audience. Social media, particularly Instagram, played a key role
in our efforts, allowing us to reach diverse communities with visually compelling posts, stories,
and educational content.Through this platform, we shared facts, videos, and interviews with
language experts to spark interest and raise awareness. In addition to our digital efforts, we
designed eye-catching posters that were distributed in schools, community centers,
and public spaces.
These posters highlighted the significance of preserving indigenous languages and
encouraged people to get involved in local language revitalization projects.
Together, these efforts helped create a broader conversation around the importance of safeguarding
indigenous linguistic heritage.
