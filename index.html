The Minds Behind This Project

Aim

Indigenous languages are disappearing at an alarming rate, taking with them irreplaceable cultural knowledge and heritage.
By starting our journey on the road to raising awareness and fostering appreciation for these languages, we hope to contribute to global efforts
to protect and revitalize them. Our project is a small step towards a larger goal of cultural preservation and respect for linguistic diversity.
